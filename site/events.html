The Suited Barber

Twenty years in the trade — now it travels.

Ben cuts on the Blacksmith floor and runs The Suited Barber — a luxury mobile service built around one idea: experience over speed. He manages the whole morning, not just the haircut, so grooms and groomsmen start the day calm, sharp and camera-ready.
