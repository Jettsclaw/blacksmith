How it works

Education is what we’re known for.

Blacksmith is an industry leader in barber education on the northern Gold Coast. Here’s how a student becomes a barber under our roof.

01

On the live floor

You train in the room, not a back classroom. From day one you’re around real cuts, real clients and the pace of a working shop.

02

Mentored by working barbers

Every student is paired with barbers who are on the tools every day. You watch it done right, then do it with someone right beside you.

03

Real certification, real clients

You finish with a recognised qualification — earned on real heads, not mannequins. Walk out ready to hold a chair of your own.

What you’ll learn

From first fade to a chair of your own.

We don’t teach theory and hope it sticks. You learn by doing, on real clients, with a mentor checking every line.

  • Clipper work, fades and tapers
  • Scissor-over-comb & texture
  • Beard shaping & hot-towel shaves
  • Consultation & reading the client
  • Shop floor pace, hygiene & chair management
