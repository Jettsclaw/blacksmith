## Type
- **Display / headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif, 500/600) — the editorial, refined feel
- **Body / UI:** Inter (400–700)
- Load both from Google Fonts.

## Colour (dark editorial + gold)
| Token | Hex | Use |

| Near-black bg | `#0d0d0f` | primary background |
| Panel bg | `#141417` | cards, alt sections |

| Muted | `#a2a2aa` | secondary text |
| Gold (accent) | `#c8a44d` | CTAs, eyebrows, key accents |
| Gold light | `#e3c578` | hovers / highlights |

## Positioning + voice
- **Line:** "More than a barbershop. We're family." Biggera Waters, Gold Coast (1 min from Harbour Town).
- **Three sides of the trade:** Barbering · Blackrose Salon Co. (the refined salon side) · The Academy (training the trade).
- **Voice:** warm, grounded, craft-proud — old-school craft in a modern room. Not flashy or corporate. "Barbers who actually know your name."
