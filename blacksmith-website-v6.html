The Shop

Ten years in the chair. The Gold Coast keeps coming back.

Blacksmith started in Biggera Waters in 2015 and never moved. Same warehouse. Same craft. Reclaimed timber, polished concrete, a cold drink in your hand and barbers who know your name before you sit down.

One minute from Harbour Town, easy parking out front, state-of-the-art chairs and an app that lets you skip the wait. We've cut more than 15,000 heads through this room — and the same barbers keep coming back. There's a reason for that.

Why people keep coming back

Stop being next in line.

The walk-in shuffle was built for the barbershop, not for you. You rock up, take a number, get whoever's free, then re-explain "short on the sides, bit of length on top" to a stranger — and walk out hoping it looks like last time. That's not a haircut. That's a gamble.

Blacksmith is the end of that. One barber. Your barber. Booked ahead on the app — no waiting, no re-explaining, no surprises.
