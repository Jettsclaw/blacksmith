Our Story

More than a barbershop — we are family

Blacksmith started in 2015 with a simple belief: a barbershop should feel like home. What began as one chair in Biggera Waters has grown into a warehouse destination — but the family ethos hasn't shifted an inch.

Walk-ins welcome via our app queue, or lock in your preferred barber with a booking. Either way, you leave sharper than you walked in.

"Built on loyalty. Run by family."
