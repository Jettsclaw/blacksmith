Blackrose Salon

Precision Cuts & Colour

Blackrose Salon sits inside Blacksmith — a dedicated space for precision hairdressing. Whether it's a sharp restyle, lived-in balayage, or full colour transformation, Blackrose delivers salon-quality results in a space that feels nothing like a typical salon.

The same warehouse energy, the same obsession with craft — designed for anyone who wants precision without pretension.

✂️

Precision Cuts

Structured bobs, textured layers, sharp lines — cut to complement your features.

🎨

Colour Services

Full colour, highlights, balayage, toner refresh — from subtle to statement.

💎

Styling & Treatments

Blowouts, event styling, and repair treatments to bring damaged hair back to life.

The Academy

Forging the Next Generation

The Blacksmith Academy is where raw talent meets real-world training. We don't just teach barbering — we build careers. Our students learn on a live shop floor alongside working professionals, graduating with the skills, confidence, and certification to walk into any shop ready to earn.

We believe the best barbers are forged in the fire of a real shop — not just a classroom.

🔨

Hands-On Training

Learn in a working barbershop environment from day one. Real clients, real pressure, real growth.

📜

Certification

Graduate with nationally recognised qualifications that open doors across the industry.

🤝

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance from experienced barbers who've built careers and businesses from the chair up.

🚀

Career Pathways

From apprentice to senior barber to shop owner — we map the journey and support every step.

📅

Flexible Programs

Monthly hands-on programs, in-house workshops, after-hours sessions, boot camps, and up-skilling for hairdressers transitioning into barbering.

⚡

Live Shop Floor

No simulated environments. You learn surrounded by 14 working operators — the real pace, the real standard.

The Experience

Where Warehouse Meets Premium

Walk in and feel it immediately — raw concrete, exposed steel, and the kind of industrial edge that can't be faked. Then sit down and experience the opposite: massage chairs, complimentary drinks, and service that treats every visit like an event.

Blacksmith isn't styled to look tough. It is tough — and refined. The warehouse aesthetic is real because the building is real. The premium finishes are deliberate because the experience matters.

💺

Massage Chairs

Complimentary Drinks
