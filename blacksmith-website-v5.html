Our Story

Barbers who actually know your name

Blacksmith started in 2015 with a simple belief: a barbershop should feel like home. What began as one chair in Biggera Waters has grown into a warehouse destination — but the family ethos hasn't shifted.

Three sides of the trade live under one roof. The barbershop. Blackrose Salon. The Academy. Different crafts, same standard — and the same crew who remember how you like your fade.

"Built on loyalty. Run by family."
